Our classes

We have a range of class styles at brighton beach yoga


Choose from vinyasa flow, gentle flow and active flow. Whatever your need, we have a class style for you. Find out more below.

With having our classes by the beach, they incorporate the natural elements and sounds of the sea, enhancing the experience of connection and relaxation.

Vinyasa flow

Vinyasa flow yoga is a dynamic style of yoga where poses are seamlessly linked together with breath, creating a flowing movement.

Gentle flow

Gentle flow yoga is a soothing and accessible style that focuses on gentle movements, breath awareness, and relaxation.

Active flow

Active flow yoga is an invigorating style that combines fluid and faster movement working with your breath, promoting strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.
