why brighton beach yoga?

Welcome to brighton beach yoga.


Born from a deep love for the sea and a desire to create inner strength, our founders, lifelong residents of Brighton, embarked on a mission to blend the beauty of coastal living with the practice of yoga.


Immersed in the bustling city, they yearned for the grounding presence of the ocean, leading them to pursue training in yoga and mindful movement. In 2019, their dream became a reality as brighton beach yoga opened its doors, offering a sanctuary for those seeking balance and connection.


Join us amidst the rhythm of the waves as we guide you through a practice that honors the body, mind, and spirit. Whether you're a seasoned yogi or taking your first steps onto the mat, our studio welcomes you to experience the transformative power of yoga by the sea.
